# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Launchy respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal data.

## Data Access
Launchy only accesses TikTok data that the user explicitly authorizes through TikTok’s official OAuth process.

## Data Usage
Authorized data is used solely to provide core app functionality such as content planning, workflow organization, and basic performance insights.

## Data Storage
Launchy does not store TikTok credentials and does not sell or share user data with third parties.

## User Control
Users may revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings.

## Compliance
Launchy complies with TikTok’s Developer Policies and applicable data protection regulations.
